About Me

Hello! I'm Philip, and I started developing for the web at a startup in NYC in 2007. Fast-forward ten years and here I am in Portland where I lead all development at Syncta as our Sr. Product Manager. Syncta is a startup that was recently acquired by Watts Water Technologies.

This site you're reading now is made with Rails 5, Bootstrap 4, and PostgreSQL. It's deployed to Microsoft Azure using Docker & Kubernetes, Let's Encrypt, HAProxy, Nginx, and Puma. You can view the source on GitLab.

My Career

I started in tech as an analyst at Brightidea back in 2007 when I was 25. At that time, I was transitioning away from mathematics and education. I taught myself Ruby on Rails and was hired a year later at readMedia as their first full-time developer. We grew the company and turned it into what Merit is today.

I now lead all development at Syncta as our Sr. Product Manager in Portland, OR. We provide mobile software for backflow testers and water purveyors. We were recently acquired by Watts Water Technologies. My passion has always been to build great products with great technologies.
